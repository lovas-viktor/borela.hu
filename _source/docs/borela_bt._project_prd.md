# Project Brief: Borela BT. Digital Presence

## 1. Project Overview
Borela BT. is a Hungarian precision engineering and CNC contract machining firm founded in 1953. The project aims to establish a high-authority digital presence that reflects "Western-European quality at Eastern-European cost." The target audience is procurement engineers and technical leads at Tier-1 automotive and industrial OEMs (e.g., Knorr-Bremse, Mercedes-Benz).

## 2. Brand Identity & Design System
The visual language follows a "German Engineering Catalogue" aesthetic—utilitarian, high-density, and authoritative.

*   **Design Principle:** Industrial precision. No marketing fluff. Numbers-backed evidence.
*   **Typography:** 
    *   **Public Sans:** Headlines and body text for high readability and neutrality.
    *   **JetBrains Mono:** Labels, technical data, and tabular numerals to evoke code and engineering precision.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary Ink (#0A0A0A):** Used for typography and high-contrast sections.
    *   **Safety Orange (#C24F1A):** Used strictly as a functional accent for primary CTAs and critical data highlights.
    *   **Neutral Surface (#F2F2F2):** The baseline background color.
*   **Visual Style:** 
    *   1px hairline dividers.
    *   2px maximum corner radii (strictly rectangular).
    *   No shadows, no gradients, no transparency (except for specific photography overlays).
    *   Monochrome documentary photography of the factory floor and technical components.

## 3. Core Components & Technical Specs
*   **Official Logo:** Typographic lockup with "BORELA." (Public Sans Black) and a burnt orange period, with a monospace tagline "PRECISION CNC / EST. 1953".
*   **Navigation:** Minimalist header with a mobile hamburger menu and a persistent "GET QUOTE" CTA.
*   **Footer:** High-density 4-column layout (Company, Services, Industries, Contact) in JetBrains Mono.

## 4. Required Content Sections (The 11 Pillars)
1.  **Hero:** Immersive workshop photography with high-impact introductory copy.
2.  **Trust Strip:** Horizontally scrollable list of Tier-1 OEM partners (Knorr-Bremse, Mercedes-Benz, etc.).
3.  **Manufacturing Services (§ 01 — CAPABILITIES):** 2x3 grid (mobile) or 3x2 grid (desktop) of specific capabilities (CNC Turning, Milling, etc.).
4.  **Metrics (§ 02 — SCALE & CAPACITY):** Dark inverse panel showing 6 key KPIs with data-display typography.
5.  **Industries (§ 03):** Specialized sectors served (Automotive, Railway, etc.).
6.  **Certifications (§ 04):** Compliance documentation (ISO 9001, 14001, VDA 6.3).
7.  **Why Borela (§ 05):** Core competitive advantages (70+ years heritage, EU production base).
8.  **Case Study (§ 06):** Featured technical project (e.g., Knorr-Bremse brake systems).
9.  **Technical FAQ (§ 07):** Collapsible engineering specifications.
10. **Closing CTA:** Final "Ready to discuss your project?" prompt with drawing upload capability.
11. **Footer:** Comprehensive legal, contact, and structural links.

## 5. Technical Constraints
*   **Company Name:** Always "BORELA BT."
*   **Copyright:** © 2026.
*   **Responsive Strategy:** Mobile-first, with specific adaptations for 1440px desktop viewports (e.g., 2-column split hero, single-row metrics).
*   **Copy Tone:** Terse, factual, and engineering-centric. Zero marketing adjectives.
