§ 07 — FREQUENT QUESTIONS

Frequent questions

  • 01 Should I use this form or the RFQ form?

    Use this form for general questions, audit requests, or finding the right contact. If you already have a drawing and want a price, the RFQ form is faster — it lets you upload the file and specs in one step.

  • 02 Which site should I ship parts to or visit?

    It depends on the project — most machining and press work runs through our Jakabszállás plant. Contact us first and we'll confirm the correct site and shipping address for your order.

  • 03 Do you reply in English and German?

    Yes. Our sales and project contacts work in Hungarian, English and German.

  • 04 Can I arrange a plant visit or a supplier audit?

    Yes — send a message with your preferred dates and the scope of the audit, and we'll arrange access to the relevant site.
