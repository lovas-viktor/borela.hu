§ 02 — WHO WE ARE

A family-owned precision shop in central Hungary

Borela is a family-owned precision machining company in central Hungary. We turn and mill components for automotive, commercial-vehicle, power-tool, sealing and general-engineering OEMs across Europe.

What has not changed in seven decades is the way we work: the same family, the same engineering culture. For a buyer, that continuity means a supplier relationship that does not reset with every quarterly strategy.

Our location is part of the offer. Sitting in the heart of the EU — no customs border, short logistics — we deliver Western-European quality at Eastern-European cost.

§ 04 — OUR STORY

Seven decades, one family, one workshop floor that never stopped growing

  1. 1953

    The first workshop

    Lajos Bognár founds his machine workshop. This is where Borela's 70+ years of engineering heritage begin.

  2. 1990

    Borela Bt. is founded

    The family workshop continues as an independent company, based in Izsák.

  3. 1996

    ISO 9001

    Borela's quality management system is certified — the foundation of its move into series production.

  4. 2005

    ISO 14001

    Environmental management certification — increasingly expected by Western-European buyers.

  5. 2007

    Knorr-Bremse Product Safety Audit

    Borela passes Knorr-Bremse's strict product safety audit — the entry point to brake-system supply.

  6. 2008

    The Fülöpszállás site

    A second site is added to expand capacity.

  7. 2009

    Supplier of the Year

    Borela is named Knorr-Bremse Supplier of the Year.

  8. 2010

    Entrepreneur of the Year

    National entrepreneurial recognition.

  9. 2016

    VDA 6.3 audit

    Borela's process audit meets the VDA 6.3 automotive standard.

  10. 2019

    Mercedes-Benz engineering project

    An engineering collaboration begins with Mercedes-Benz Manufacturing Hungary.

  11. 2020s

    New hall & focused production

    A new production hall opens in Jakabszállás. Borela moves heat treatment to a specialized partner so it can concentrate on its core machining expertise.

  12. Today

    A precision partner trusted by European OEMs

    50 people, 3 sites, 40 CNC lathes and 6 machining centers — a precision partner trusted by European OEMs.

§ 05 — WHAT DEFINES US

Four things that have not changed in 70 years

01

Continuity

The same family, the same engineering culture, decade after decade. For a buyer, that means a supplier relationship that does not depend on a quarterly strategy.

02

Engineering-led

Decisions are made by engineers, not by sales. That is why we tell you plainly what we can and cannot do — for example, we coordinate grinding and heat treatment through trusted partners rather than overpromise.

03

Transparency

Realistic tolerances, realistic lead times, realistic capabilities. Our ±0.01 mm typical tolerance is stated honestly — tighter work is arranged with a partner, not hidden.

04

Single point of contact

We run the full project management, including coordination of outsourced heat treatment. You sign one contract and have one contact.

§ 10 — COMMON QUESTIONS

Common questions about Borela

  • 01 How long has Borela been operating?

    Borela's engineering heritage goes back to a machine workshop founded in 1953. The company has held ISO 9001 certification since 1996 and has served automotive and engineering OEMs across Europe for decades.

  • 02 Is Borela a family business?

    Yes. Borela is family-owned and engineering-led — the same family and the same engineering culture have run the company throughout its history.

  • 03 Where is Borela located?

    Borela operates three sites in central Hungary: headquarters in Izsák, the primary machining and pressing plant in Jakabszállás (3,000 m²), and a secondary machining plant in Fülöpszállás (2,000 m²).

  • 04 How many people work at Borela?

    Around 50 people across three sites, operating 40 CNC lathes and 6 machining centers.
