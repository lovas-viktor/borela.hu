§ 08 — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
  • What industries does Borela serve?

    We supply automotive OEMs and tier-1 suppliers, commercial-vehicle braking systems, power tool manufacturers, sealing technology, and general mechanical engineering. Long-standing customers include Knorr-Bremse, Mercedes-Benz, Hilti, Freudenberg, Sulzer, Phoenix Mecano and Zarges.

  • What series sizes do you produce?

    Typical orders run from 500 to 100,000 pieces. We also produce validation samples and pre-series before committing to volume tooling.

  • What tolerances can you hold?

    Typical tolerance is ±0.01 mm, verified on in-house CMM and contour measurement equipment with micro-hardness testing where required.

  • Are you certified for automotive supply?

    Yes. ISO 9001 (since 1996), ISO 14001 (since 2005), and VDA 6.3 process audit (maintained since 2016). We also passed the Knorr-Bremse Product Safety Audit in 2007.
